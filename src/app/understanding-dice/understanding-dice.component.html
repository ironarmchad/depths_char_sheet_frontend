Understanding Dice Probabilities

Probably the most fun part of Depths is the amount of creativity that it allows; there is no in-depth rule book nor set classes nor spells nor even standard attack mechanics. The problem is that the balance of the game depends on the ability of the story teller to read a player written mechanic and understand what the consequence will be. Thus here is my guide to dice and their uses in the Depths.

Success/Failure Mechanics

The most common mechanic in Depths is the binary outcome roll. For the player this almost always means roll 1d6 and add a stat to it, but the situation may demand different mechanics.

Critical/Botch Rolls

In the Depths, all Success/Failure rolls are subject to criticals (a roll of 6 on 1d6 dice) or botch (a roll of 1 on 1d6 dice). Criticals are automatic successes and often come with extra rewards, whereas a botch is an automatic failure usually with extra consequences.
